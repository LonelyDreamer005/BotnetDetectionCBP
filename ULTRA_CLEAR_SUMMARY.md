## 📋 The 3 Questions You Might Have

### 1. What did we actually build?
We built an **AI Guard**.
- It sits on a network and watches data move.
- It can tell the difference between a **Human** (Benign) and a **Botnet** (Syn, LDAP, UDP attacks).

### 2. Why did we need 4 notebooks?
Because we were doing a **Scientific Investigation**:
*   **Notebook 1**: We built the basic AI.
*   **Notebook 2**: We investigated "Why is the accuracy so high?" (The Leakage Audit).
*   **Notebook 3**: We tested if the AI works in the real world (The Generalization Test).
*   **Notebook 4**: We combined everything to make the "Ultimate Version" (The Universal Classifier).

### 3. Why is the "Universal" version the best?
Imagine a student who only studies the practice exam. On test day, if the questions are slightly different, they fail.
- **Our first AI** was that student.
- **Our Universal AI** studied 10 different textbooks and practice exams. It now understands the actual *subject*, not just the specific questions.
